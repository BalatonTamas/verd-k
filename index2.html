Koenigsegg 1:1

The iconic Koenigsegg One:1 was the first homologated production car in the world a power-to-weight ratio of 1:1 - the 'dream' ratio previously thought impossible in a road-legal sports car. Hence the name One:1. And with one megawatt of power, it was also the world's first production megacar.

Apollo ie

The Apollo Intensa Emozione, sometimes referred as the IE, is a mid-engine sports car manufactured by German automobile manufacturer Apollo Automobil GmbH, in partnership with Manifattura Automobili Torino, designed by their chief designer Jowyn Wong. Intensa Emozione means 'intense emotion' in Italian.

Laferrari

LaFerrari, project name F150, is a limited production mid-engine, mild hybrid sports car built by Italian automotive manufacturer Ferrari. LaFerrari means "The Ferrari" in Italian, meaning this is supposed to be the definitive Ferrari.
